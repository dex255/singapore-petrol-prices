About Singapore Petrol Prices

Petrol prices in Singapore are adjusted periodically by major fuel brands including Esso, Shell, SPC, Caltex, and Sinopec. This visual comparison table helps motorists find the best value for their vehicle, whether you use 92, 95, 98-octane petrol, premium grades, or diesel.

Save more by combining these pump prices with various credit card promotions and loyalty programmes. Prices are updated daily based on information from motorist.sg to ensure you always have the latest rates at your fingertips.
